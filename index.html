Senjumaru Shutara — Bankai
Loom of Death
Passive
Per-Hank Passives
Hank 1 → Buffs Umbrella counter speed + reduces endlag Hank 2 → Increases posture and defense Hank 3 → Slows targets, prevents flash-stepping Hank 4 → Blinds and slows with snow / ice Hank 5 → Burn as strong as Flame Bankai Hank 6 → Siphons rei back to user (% lowers with more people in room)
Shikai Passive — Wardrobe Malfunction
After dealing enough damage with skills, the opponent's clothing malfunctions — spikes erupt from under their clothes.
Base Moves
T — Loom of Death
Forms a cloth room. Use again → random room at high rei cost. Hold button to return to room 1.
G — Unravel (1–6 Hanks)
Activates the current room's special ability. Cannot move while chanting.
Z — Catch Stitch
Throws needle connected to reiatsu as a pull-in move.
X — Running Stitch
Dash / movement move.
C — Umbrella Counter
Sews an umbrella as a counter. On hit: fabric bursts at attacker, binding their arms for X seconds. Similar to Quilge crit.
Hanks
Hank 1 — Eye Retaliation
Taking damage here deals it back to the attacker. "The eyes be crushed, of those who stare back."
Hank 2 — Iron Maiden
Iron maiden of cloth crushes anyone inside. Guard breaks. "There shall be nothing to stand dressed in."
Hank 3 — Sand Pit
Drags targets to centre, constant tick damage. "To be pulled in so shall be fatal."
Hank 4 — Freeze
Freezes targets over time, constant tick damage. "To enter a place in which never to wake again."
Hank 5 — Inferno
Ignites all trees at once. Massive explosion. "Until turned to ashes, there shall be no escape."
Hank 6 — Death Star Drain
Constant health drain, small % returned to user. % decreases with more people in room. "To be caught in the end, by the star of death."
Senjumaru — Shikai
Shigarami · Rare or Legendary
Passive
Threads
Gain threads by landing moves. Threads change how Z and X behave. C (Spindle) is excluded — it is the counter only. Landing X grants threads.
Moves
Z — Distaff
Barrage attack with the needle. Like Shinso / Snow / Suzumebachi. Threads modify its behaviour.
X — Puncture
Short dash, stabs needle into target. Press again in a few seconds (like Blood Z) → burst damage + stun. Landing X grants threads.
C — Spindle
Needle transforms into umbrella. Spin to deflect damage back at the attacker. Pressure shift / parry. C is never affected by threads.
Tempest — Gonryōmaru
Chojiro Sasakibe Shikai · Mythical
Passive
Storm Conduction
Land Tempest moves or perfect dodge → gain Shock Charges (max 6, +2 per move). 1–2 charges → ~5–10% speed buff 3–4 charges → bigger dodge window 4–6 charges → further flashsteps (possible new VFX) Visual: yellow current balls orbit the user as a charge indicator.
Base Moves
Z — Tempest Rush
3 zig-zag dashes with the blade. Caught targets are dragged through the move. Ends with horizontal knockback slash (or kick). ⚡ 1 Charge 4 dashes, faster animation = less reaction time.
X — Vivid Volt / Volt Sever
Zangerin projectile forward. On hit: teleport above → fierce vertical down-slam. ⚡ 2 Charges — Bola Voltio Two current balls fuse into a tick-damage beam. 100% posture. Shares cooldown.
C — Thunder Time
Tracking thunderstorm above opponent. Occasional lightning bolts force block or shock. Constant pressure — like Chess / Ghost.
Mode — Kōkō Gonryō Rikyū
Pop — Raise to the Heavens
Raises sword to the sky. Lightning shower falls across the area, converting surroundings into a Thunderstorm. Thunderstorm can be consumed by G for focused lightning.
T — Kōkō Gonryō Rikyū
The only Bankai to ever leave a scar on Yamamoto. Lightning cage on self. Bends the bars and rushes forward absorbing it all. Heavy damage + Bleed on anyone caught. Deals 2 bars.
G — Sky's Judgement
Absorbs the Thunderstorm into sword. Points at mouse location and fires a concentrated lightning bolt of immense force. Storm disappears. Deals 1–2 bars.
Senjumaru — TYPE://SOUL
Shigarami Full Design · @Ezzotik
Passive — Most Important
Tether Hands
Start with 1 hand. Land Shigarami moves → gain hands (max 4). More hands = more damage across all moves. Hands are destroyed when hit — like the Zombie mechanic. Losing hands scales damage back down.
Base Moves
Z — Needle Throw
Simultaneous needle barrage. Close range = more concentrated damage. Base: 4 needles +2 needles per extra hand Max (4 hands): 10 needles
X — Hundred Robe Prison
Weave hands in front. On land: target wrapped in cloth, kicked far, stunned ~1s before breaking free. More hands = longer stun + more damage.
C — Weaving Rush
Wide, fast claw-rush. More hands = bigger AOE + more damage. Consumes 1 hand on use. At 1 hand: cannot use (or no consumption — TBD).
Mode — K: Thousand Robe Death
T — Thread Guillotine
Thin horizontal thread across the battlefield. More hands = thicker thread / wider hitbox.
G — Needle Mirage
Creates silk clones that can imitate all abilities. Moderate health. 1 hand = 1 clone, up to 4.
Z — Cloth Reprimand
Mouse-aimable. Teleports user to target and covers the surrounding area in cloth.
X — String Umbrella
Silk umbrella that repels and reflects all incoming attacks. Full parry / reflect tool.
C — Weaving Rush
Unchanged from base. Still costs a hand. Still scales with hand count.
Ultimate — Thousand Robe Death
Thousand Robe Death
User unleashes full Loom authority. Battlefield becomes a Dressing Ritual — every enemy is treated as a mannequin being fitted with a divine outfit. Each garment is a different method of execution. • Players sent to individual garment rooms • Z (in ult): teleport between rooms • X (in ult): trigger that room's special ability • C (in ult): TBD — to be designed Connects structurally to Bankai Loom of Death's hank room system.
